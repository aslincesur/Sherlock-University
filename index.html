About Our Institution

Welcome to a university where logic, deduction, and high-stakes academia collide. At Baker Street University, we cultivate the brilliant minds needed to navigate the complicated mazes of modern society. With a focus on acute detail and applied criminology, we unravel academia's most perplexing theories.
